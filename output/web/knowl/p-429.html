One example of a data visualization using the internet is a video which shows multiple different graphs. Sometimes these visualizations will just be a series of graphs like the ones we’ve seen before. For example, the graph in Figure 4.6.21 showed the relationship between the gross domestic product (GDP) and CO2 emissions per person for countries in 2018. The video in Figure 4.6.31 shows the same data, but for every year from 1961 to 2014. It also adds another data set - how much the temperature in that country has gone up or down from the average over 1961-2018. The temperature change is represented by the color and size of the dot.
