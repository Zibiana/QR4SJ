Access to data is itself a social justice issue. For example, researchers over the last decade have monitored public Twitter posts to analyze how people feel about various issues (a process called "sentiment analysis") or generally what people are talking about. Previously, access to \(1\%\) of tweets on the platform had been free through Twitter’s API, a collection of methods for accessing Twitter’s data. However, in March 2023, Elon Musk and other Twitter decision-makers raised the price for access to \(0.3\%\) of tweets to $42,000 a month 122 , a cost that one researcher said no "academic on the planet...could afford."
